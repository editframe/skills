## Connect Waveform to Audio or Video

**Problem:** You want to visualize audio from an audio or video element with a waveform.

**Solution:** Use the `target` attribute on the waveform element, referencing the `id` of the audio or video element.

<Demonstration layout="horizontal" sourceVisible alwaysShowSource enablePreview enableFilmstrip enableFitScale>
  <Timegroup mode="contain" className="aspect-[1/1] w-[500px] h-[500px] bg-black flex flex-col items-center justify-center">
    <Audio src="https://assets.editframe.com/card-joker.mp3" id="connect-audio" />
    <Waveform target="connect-audio" mode="bars" className="w-full h-[200px] block" />
  </Timegroup>
</Demonstration>

**Steps:**

1. Add an `id` attribute to your `<ef-audio>` or `<ef-video>` element
2. Set the `target` attribute on `<ef-waveform>` to match the `id` value
3. The waveform will automatically synchronize with the target element's playback

The waveform receives real-time audio frequency data from the target element and visualizes it during playback. For deeper understanding of how this connection works, see the [Real-Time Visualization explanation](/docs/elements/waveform/explanation/real-time-visualization).
