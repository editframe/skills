## Tutorial: Your First Thumbnail Strip

This tutorial walks you through creating thumbnail strips step by step. Each step builds on the previous one, teaching you how to generate visual previews of video content.

### Step 1: Basic Thumbnail Strip

Connect a thumbnail strip to a video element to display thumbnails automatically.

**What you'll learn:** How to connect a thumbnail strip to a video element and display thumbnails automatically.

<Demonstration layout="horizontal" sourceVisible alwaysShowSource enablePreview enableFilmstrip enableFitScale>
  <div className="flex flex-col gap-4">
    <Video 
      id="tutorial-video-1"
      src="https://assets.editframe.com/bars-n-tone.mp4" 
      className="aspect-[16/9]" 
    />
    <ThumbnailStrip 
      target="tutorial-video-1" 
      thumbnailWidth={80}
      className="h-16 rounded border border-gray-600"
    />
  </div>
</Demonstration>

**Try it yourself:** Change the `thumbnailWidth` attribute to `120` and notice how fewer, larger thumbnails are displayed.

### Step 3: Custom Time Ranges

Show thumbnails for a specific portion of the video using custom time ranges.

**What you'll learn:** How to use `startTimeMs` and `endTimeMs` to display thumbnails from a specific time window.

<Demonstration layout="horizontal" sourceVisible alwaysShowSource enablePreview enableFilmstrip enableFitScale>
  <div className="flex flex-col gap-4">

**Try it yourself:** Adjust the `startTimeMs` and `endTimeMs` values to show thumbnails from different portions of the video.

### Step 4: Intrinsic vs Trimmed Duration

Explore how thumbnail strips handle trimmed videos and intrinsic duration modes.

**What you'll learn:** How `useIntrinsicDuration` affects thumbnail generation when videos are trimmed.

<Demonstration layout="horizontal" sourceVisible alwaysShowSource enablePreview enableFilmstrip enableFitScale>
  <div className="flex flex-col gap-4">
    <Video 
      id="tutorial-video-4"
      src="https://assets.editframe.com/bars-n-tone.mp4" 
      trimstart="2s"
      trimend="2s"
      className="aspect-[16/9]" 
    />
    <ThumbnailStrip 
      target="tutorial-video-4" 
      thumbnailWidth={80}
      className="h-16 rounded border border-gray-600"
    />
    <ThumbnailStrip 
      target="tutorial-video-4" 
      thumbnailWidth={80}
      useIntrinsicDuration
      className="h-16 rounded border border-gray-600"
    />
  </div>
</Demonstration>

**Try it yourself:** Compare the two strips - the first shows thumbnails from the trimmed portion, while the second shows thumbnails from the entire source video.

### Step 5: Responsive Layout

See how thumbnail strips automatically adapt to different container widths.

**What you'll learn:** How thumbnail strips calculate the optimal number of thumbnails based on available width.

<Demonstration layout="horizontal" sourceVisible alwaysShowSource enablePreview enableFilmstrip enableFitScale>

    </div>
  </div>
</Demonstration>

**Try it yourself:** Resize your browser window and watch how the thumbnail strips automatically adjust the number of thumbnails to fit the available width.
