## Video Element

The video element enables playback and manipulation of video content in Editframe compositions. It supports both development preview (using `src` with JIT transcoding) and production rendering (using `assetId` with uploaded assets).

Video elements can be used standalone or combined with other elements in timegroups to create complex compositions. They support trimming, effects, audio analysis, and precise timing control.
