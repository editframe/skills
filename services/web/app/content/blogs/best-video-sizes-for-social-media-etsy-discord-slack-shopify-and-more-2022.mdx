## Best Video Sizes for Social Media Online

We wrote a quick, up-to-date video size guide for posting videos on social media. We included all of the formats, frame rates, limits, and everything else you might need to quickly understand for 2022 when it comes to video.

If you plan to upload a video directly to some of the most popular platforms used today, you want to get a few things right before generating your video. You don’t want to be in a scenario where you upload a file that is too large, too long, or just exported in the wrong format. Across all platforms, there are a few different ways to upload and a handful of details to consider.



We also distinguished between either an organic video upload to a network or a video advertisement that you upload for promotion. Some of these platforms don’t support video advertisements, so we don’t include those in this updated guide.



### **Discord Video Sizes**

Discord is a common channel to communicate if you’re a musician, gamer, into crypto, or just someone who uses Discord as a primary tool for communication with your friends. Although you can share large files, Discord allows you to share certain videos that embed directly in your chat versus the alternative of uploading to a third party like YouTube or Vimeo.

**Discord Upload Video Embed Guide**

\- Discord Video Upload Embed

*   Format: MP4, .MOV (some devices), WebM , .GIF
*   Max Size: 8MB, 50MB with Nitro

### **Slack Video Sizes**

Similar to Discord, at work, communication sometimes involves uploading a video to share with a colleague. Luckily, Slack allows members of groups to share pretty large file sizes seamlessly across channels.

**Slack Upload Video Embed Guide**

\- Slack Video Upload Embed

*   Embeddable Format: MP4, .MOV
*   Non-Embeddable Format: WMV, AVI, WebM, Vp8
*   Max Size: 1GB

### **Shopify Video Sizes**

Shopify is a wonderful place to upload videos about your products. They have a pretty easy-to-follow guideline of how many videos you can upload and generally the limitations of the resolution available for each video. Your Shopify plan defines your video limits, and most likely, if you’re a Shopify Plus user, the team would be pretty accommodating to your needs.

There currently aren’t any “Video Ad” formats in the product, so right now, it is just product videos.

**Shopify Video Upload Sizes**

\- Uploaded Videos on Shopify

*   Video Resolution : Up to 4k or (4096 x 2160 px)
*   Video Formats: .MP4 or .MOV
*   Video Size: Up to 1GB
*   Video Length: Up to 10 Minutes

\- Shopify Limits on Videos

*   Basic Shopify: 250 Videos
*   Shopify: 1000 Videos
*   Advanced Shopify: 5000 Videos
*   Shopify: Contact Shopify Plus Support for Max

### **Etsy Video Sizes**

Etsy is pretty fantastic for sellers when it comes to video. They allow you to directly upload Shop Videos and Listing Videos, two different types of videos. It seems like video is becoming a more prominent component of their product roadmap for sellers.

**Etsy Video Upload Guide**

\- Upload Listing Videos on Etsy

*   Video Resolution : 1080p
*   Video Formats: .MP4, .MOV, FLV, AAC, AVI, 3GP, MPEG
*   Video Size: Up to 100MB
*   Video Length: 5 to 15 Seconds
*   Audio: Audio is removed once uploaded

\- Upload Shop Videos on Etsy

*   Video Aspect Ratio: No requirements, suggested Landscape
*   Video Formats: MOV, MPEG, AVI, MP4, M4V
*   Video Size: Up to 300MB
*   Video Length: Try not to exceed 2 MInutes, Videos that exceed 10 Minutes will experience longer upload times.
*   Caption: 120 characters max

### **TikTok Video Sizes**

There is probably no platform that is more dominant when it comes to short-form user-generated video content as of the last few years. Not only is organic content growing and dominant, but TikTok advertising revenue is thriving in this video first format. What I would watch out for is the evolution of the format when it comes to its duration and limits. Recently it has been reported that TikTok plans to extend the video duration to 10 minutes, which would be the second time it has expanded this upload limit.

**Tiktok Video Sizes and Video Ad Sizes Guide**

\- TikTok Organic In Feed Content Video

*   Minimum Resolution: 1080x1920
*   Recommended Aspect Ratio: 9:16
*   Max File Size: 287.6MB for iOS users and 72MB for Android
*   Video Formats: MP4 or MOV
*   Length Max: Previously 15 seconds recorded in-app, 60 seconds in-app (4 sections of 15 seconds), or over 60 seconds when uploaded from another source up to 3 minutes. There are confirmed sources that this maximum will go up to 10 minutes soon!

\- TikTok Video In Feed Ads

*   Minimum Resolution: 540x960, 640x64 or 960x540
*   Recommended Aspect Ratio: 9:16, 1:1, or 16:9
*   Max File Size: 500MB
*   Video Formats: .MP4, .MOV, .MPEG .3GP, .AVI
*   Length Max: 5–60 seconds (9–15 sec is recommended)
*   Description of Ad: 1-1000 Latin characters or 1-50 Asian characters
*   Bitrate minimum: 516 kbps
